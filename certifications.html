VLSI Design

  • The VLSI Design virtual internship program at Maven Silicon Softech Pvt Ltd, completed in April 2024, provided participants with in industry-standard VLSI design methodologies, focusing on Verilog and SystemVerilog projects.
  • The program aimed to equip students with essential skills for careers in the VLSI industry, covering a wide range of topics
    1. Fundamentals of VLSI Design: Introduction to the basic concepts and principles of VLSI technology, including chip architecture and design flow.
    2. Digital Design and RTL Coding: Training in Register Transfer Level (RTL) coding using Verilog and SystemVerilog, emphasizing best practices in digital design.
    3. Static Timing Analysis (STA): Learning to meet timing constraints and perform synthesis of designs using synthesizable constructs.
    4. Project Work: Participants engaged in real-world projects that simulated industry scenarios, enhancing their practical skills and understanding of VLSI design.
    5. Virtual Learning Experience: The program utilized online platforms for lectures, live Q&A sessions, and collaborative projects, allowing flexibility and accessibility for all participants.
